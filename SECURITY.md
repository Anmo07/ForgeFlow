# Security Policy (SECURITY.md)

## Vulnerability Disclosure Policy
At ForgeFlow, we take the security of our users and data extremely seriously. If you suspect or discover a security vulnerability in our platform, please report it to us immediately via our secure disclosure channel.

## How to Report
Please email security details to: **security@forgeflow.com**

Please include:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue (proof-of-concept code, requests, or screenshots).
- Any potential impact on users or data.

## Safe Harbor & Guidelines
We support responsible disclosure. Under this policy, we promise not to pursue legal action against security researchers who:
- Give us reasonable time to investigate and remediate the issue before making any public disclosure.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability (e.g. download more data than necessary to demonstrate the issue, or alter user data).
- Do not perform Denial of Service (DoS) or physical security testing.

## SLA Response Times
- **Acknowledgement**: Within 24 hours of receipt.
- **Initial Assessment & Classification**: Within 72 hours.
- **Remediation Target**: 
  - *Critical/High severity*: Resolved within 14 days.
  - *Medium/Low severity*: Resolved within 30 to 45 days.
